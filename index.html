Who Are the Imps?

The Pitt Imps are a Pittsburgh-based Premodern format Magic: the Gathering crew. We have an organized monthly meetup, ad-hoc events, and an active Discord server!

Originally known as "The Lords of the Pitt", we caught a ton of flack for that one online due to similarly named Chicago based-crew, and now we're here. Impish. Efficient. Ready to spend BB each turn. Lord of the Pit hasn't been registered in an Old School tournament in like 10 years, but who's keeping track?
